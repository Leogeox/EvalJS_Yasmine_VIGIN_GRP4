Undertale is an indie role-playing game created by Toby Fox, released in 2015. The game takes place in the underground world of monsters, where players control a child who has fallen into this realm. The unique aspect of Undertale is its "moral choice" system, where players can choose between sparing or fighting monsters. The game's outcome depends on these choices, leading to multiple possible endings. Undertale features a distinctive retro-style visual design, quirky humor, and a memorable soundtrack. Its deep narrative and player-driven decisions have made it a cult favorite among gamers.
